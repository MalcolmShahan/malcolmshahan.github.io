About Me

I'm a passionate programmer looking to make my way into the gaming industry. I have formal training in C# and Java programming, as well as self-taught skill in Python and Unity, and I am always excited to learn something new.

Growing up, I've always been fascinated with video games and the worlds that can be created within them. Through a combination of formal education, independent online courses, and hands-on trial and error, I've built up skills that will bring me closer to my goal of making my own games.

My independent studies have taught me how to be resourceful in gathering and implementing new information from both published sources and my peers in order to solve problems, and I believe the best solutions come from a combination of both raw intelligence and unique perspectives to apply knowledge in new and innovative ways.
